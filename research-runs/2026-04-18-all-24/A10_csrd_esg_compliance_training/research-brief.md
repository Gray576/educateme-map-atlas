## Immediate risk picture

1. [high] CSRD Wave 2 reporting NOW.
   Reason: The European Commission has proposed and then advanced the stop-the-clock changes that delay reporting for companies that would have reported as of 2026 or 2027 until 2028. For the product's SME segment, 'now' is materially misleading.
   Sources: ec_sustainable_finance_2025, ec_csrd_omnibus_2025, ec_company_reporting_2025
2. [high] No French or German CSRD training for Luxembourg SMEs.
   Reason: Public Luxembourg providers already offer CSRD and ESG training in French, and PwC offers CSRD double-materiality training in English, French and German.
   Sources: houseoftraining_csrd_fr, houseoftraining_vsme, houseoftraining_esg_fr, pwc_csrd_dma
