# B2 Validation Packet

Product: `B2 Medical French for Nurses`

## Objective

Turn B2 from a vague three-country concept into one country-specific route with an evidence-backed outcome path.

## Current blocked items

- product certificate acceptance
- unified three-country offer
- France workforce number
- market-gap claim
- France nurse-specific requirement pack

## First decision

Choose one wedge country before more validation:
- France
- Belgium
- Switzerland

If this is not chosen first, the product should remain blocked.

## Workstreams

### 1. Route-owner / authority validation

Questions:
- What exact language-proof or recognition path is required in the chosen country?
- Does any provider-issued course certificate help, or is the route dependent on external authority-recognised proof only?
- Which exact authority pages can be cited in public copy?

Acceptable evidence:
- official authority/helpdesk reply
- route documentation from a recognised intermediary or employer that matches the official rule
- explicit statement on whether course-attendance certificates matter or do not matter

### 2. Wedge-country demand validation

Questions:
- Do employers, recruiters, or candidates actually need this product in the chosen country?
- Is the bottleneck language proof, route navigation, interview readiness, or documentation support?

Acceptable evidence:
- 5 to 7 interviews split across employers, recruiters, and candidates
- at least 2 written signals that the narrow country-specific offer solves a real bottleneck

### 3. Market and positioning cleanup

Questions:
- Is there a real country-specific workforce-demand number that survives primary-source review?
- Is there a dedicated medical-French gap, or only a broader language-training market with substitutes?

Acceptable evidence:
- primary-source workforce or shortage data
- narrow competitor scan for the chosen country and buyer

## Pass criteria

Pass to `review`:
- one wedge country chosen
- one country-specific route pack verified
- provider certificate claim removed unless directly validated
- unsupported workforce and market-gap claims removed

Pass to `launchable`:
- one recognised outcome path for the chosen country
- one pilot, LOI, or equivalent written demand signal for the country-specific offer

Kill trigger:
- insistence on selling one France-Belgium-Switzerland narrative without country-specific redesign
