## What survived

1. Germany is the only verified jurisdiction for this rerun.
2. The core route context is real: Germany generally requires B2 German for nursing work, while some nursing-trainee routes can require B1 or B2 depending on the federal state.
3. The exam-route context is real: telc Deutsch B1-B2 Pflege and Goethe-Test PRO Pflege both exist as nursing-specific proof routes.
4. The strongest official need signal is real: the Federal Employment Agency reported 35,000 average nursing vacancies in 2023.
5. The strongest safe foreign-worker figures are 270,000 and 16% in 2023, then 18% in 2025.
6. Public ecosystem routes exist: BAMF occupational German support exists, and Triple Win is a real public nursing-recruitment route for a narrower country set than the legacy copy claimed.
