# Blocked Products Decision Memo

Run: `2026-04-18-all-24`  
Scope: the 7 products that remain `blocked` after the strict `research-v2` rerun

## Decision thresholds

`blocked -> review`
- Enough public or directly documented evidence exists to remove the current unsafe claims.
- Remaining copy is narrow, source-tied, and free of material contradictions.
- Unknowns can stay unknown, but no blocked claim is still presented as fact.

`review -> launchable`
- The route is not only fact-safe, but commercially validated.
- There is buyer-side proof that the packaging, price, and delivery model work in practice.
- Required partner, accreditation, or certificate paths are confirmed in writing, not inferred.
