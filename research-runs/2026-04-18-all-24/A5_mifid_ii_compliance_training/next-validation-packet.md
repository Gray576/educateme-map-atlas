# A5 Validation Packet

Product: `A5 MiFID II Compliance Training`

## Objective

Resolve whether A5 has a real certificate/accreditation route and whether subsidy wording is legally safe. Without that, this is not a launchable compliance SKU.

## Current blocked items

- product-specific accreditation route
- subsidy eligibility copy
- deadline copy
- audience-size copy
- competitor-gap copy

## Workstreams

### 1. Route-owner validation

Questions:
- Can this product issue a certificate that buyers may treat as satisfying a real MiFID II/CSSF training requirement?
- If not, can the product be delivered under a listed or accredited partner route?
- What exact wording is allowed in customer-facing copy for certificate/accreditation status?

Acceptable evidence:
- written response from a CSSF-listed provider, accreditation owner, or equivalent route owner
- sample certificate or programme description showing the legal/operational route
- partner confirmation that EducateMe can deliver under that route

### 2. Subsidy/legal wording validation

Questions:
- Does Luxembourg company-training co-funding apply to this exact offer despite the regulated-profession exclusion language?
- If the answer is conditional, what exact conditions must be stated in copy?

Acceptable evidence:
- written reply from Guichet, INFPC, or an equivalent official administrator
- written legal/compliance note from a qualified local advisor tied to the cited official rule

### 3. Buyer validation

Questions:
- Do supervised firms need an accredited certificate, or is a competence-refresh format sufficient?
- Would they buy this offer at the intended price if it is positioned as prep rather than as an accredited course?

Acceptable evidence:
- 3 to 5 buyer conversations with compliance, L&D, or business-line owners
- at least 1 written follow-up showing willingness to pilot or buy

## Pass criteria

Pass to `review`:
- certificate/accreditation wording is resolved in writing
- subsidy wording is either validated or removed
- deadline, audience-size, and competitor-gap claims are either evidenced or deleted

Pass to `launchable`:
- one real buyer pilot, LOI, or paid deal
- final public copy matches the confirmed accreditation and subsidy route exactly

Kill trigger:
- no defensible certificate/accreditation route and no buyer demand for a non-certifying prep format
