# A9 Product Brief

## What it is

`A9` is an `Integration Bundle (Sproochtest+)` around the Luxembourg nationality and integration route.
It sits between language prep, route navigation, and civic-integration support.

## Who it is for

- clearest user: nationality candidates in Luxembourg
- secondary but unverified angle: HR/onboarding support for newcomers

## Problem it tries to solve

People do not just need “a language course”.
They need a clear route:

- what to pass
- what certificate is recognized
- what civic component is required
- what reimbursement or approval rules apply

## What survived fact-checking

- naturalisation requires both language proof and `Vivre ensemble`
- the official language route is `INLL Sproochentest`
- the official integration route already exists
- the clean reimbursement layer is narrower than legacy copy
- real alternatives already exist:
  - INLL / SFA route
  - ETIC
  - Prolingua
  - Lux'Languages
  - LuxLessons

## What is still blocked

- the exact A9 bundle boundary
- any A9-specific approval or reimbursement eligibility
- any official value of an A9-issued certificate
- any single-number reimbursement-cap copy beyond the verified EUR `75` test fee
- HR-onboarding demand
- uniqueness / whitespace claims
