# A1 Product Brief

## What it is

`A1` is a Luxembourg-focused `AI Literacy Workshop` for employers.
It is positioned as a short training offer around the Article 4 AI Act literacy requirement.

## Who it is for

- primary buyer: employer / HR / L&D / compliance-adjacent teams
- likely use case: staff awareness and defensible baseline AI training

## Problem it tries to solve

Companies need a fast way to train relevant staff on AI literacy without overclaiming regulatory significance.

## What survived fact-checking

- Article 4 AI literacy framing survived in a narrow official form
- the obligation applies from `2 February 2025`
- Luxembourg company-training co-funding exists
- `Fit 4 AI` exists as a separate public support route
- `House of Training` is a verified Luxembourg competitor

## What is still blocked

- whether the workshop certificate has official or buyer-recognized compliance significance
- whether workshop documentation can be marketed as audit/compliance evidence
- whether this exact workshop qualifies for AI-specific subsidy routes
- any exclusivity or market-gap claim

## Market evidence snapshot

- release status: `review`
- quadrant segment: `B2B`
- scope class: `local`
- evidence db: `LU`
- provisional market score: `1.51`
- anchor keywords:
  - `ai training luxembourg`
  - `ai literacy training`
  - `ai act training`

## Why it is weaker than A8/A9

- the regulatory thesis is clear, but search-visible local demand is still weaker
- differentiation is not yet proven
- commercial and audit-significance claims remain blocked

## Current decision

Keep as a real option, but do not treat it as a leading Luxembourg market bet until stronger demand and buyer-proof show up.
