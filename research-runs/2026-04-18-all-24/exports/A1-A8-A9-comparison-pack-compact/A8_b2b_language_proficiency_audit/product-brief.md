# A8 Product Brief

## What it is

`A8` is a `B2B Language Proficiency Audit`.
It is closer to assessment and workforce capability mapping than to a normal language course.

## Who it is for

- primary buyer: employer / HR / L&D
- likely use case: assess current employee language capability before training, staffing, or role assignment

## Problem it tries to solve

Companies often do not have a reliable view of who can work in which language and at what level.
That makes hiring, training, and internal readiness decisions weaker.

## What survived fact-checking

- the general assessment need remains plausible
- multilingual and Luxembourg-linked assessment alternatives exist
- the absolute uniqueness story does not survive

## What is still blocked or weak

- the `78% of employers use language tests` figure has no valid source
- Luxembourg-specific uniqueness is contradicted
- several commercial assumptions remain internal hypotheses:
  - lead magnet mechanics
  - team size per audit
  - margin
  - pilot sufficiency
