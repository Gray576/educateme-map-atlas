- Title: Integration Bundle (Sproochtest+)
- Run id: 2026-04-18-all-24

## What survived

1. Luxembourg naturalisation requires both a language-test certificate and a Vivre ensemble certificate.
2. The official language route is the INLL Sproochentest at A2 speaking and B1 listening, and INLL treats it as the only recognised certification for nationality applications.
3. Luxembourg already has an official civic-integration route through the 24-hour Vivre ensemble course or exam, including online English delivery.
4. The clean reimbursement layer is narrower than the legacy copy: the official Sproochentest fee is EUR 75, and a conditional reimbursement route exists for the test and approved courses after the nationality application is filed.
5. Verified alternatives already exist, including the INLL or SFA preparatory route, ETIC, Prolingua, Lux'Languages, and LuxLessons.

## Blocking questions

1. What is the real A9 boundary: exam prep only, prep plus civic integration, or a broader HR onboarding bundle?
2. Can any part of A9 qualify as an approved or reimbursable Article 28 nationality-law course?
3. Does any A9-issued certificate or completion record have official standing in the nationality process?
4. Can the reimbursement story be safely published with a single course-fee cap, or must it stay route-level and conditional?
5. What differentiator remains once public and private alternatives are treated as real competitors rather than whitespace?

## Contradictions that stay closed

1. [high] Government reimbursement ~750 after exam pass
   Problem: the official Guichet page supports the EUR 75 test fee and a conditional reimbursement route, but the legacy line compresses course and exam rules into one simplified statement. The clean 750-euro course-cap copy did not survive as a primary-source dashboard claim in this rerun.

2. [high] No integration bundle competitor exists / pure upsell
   Problem: Luxembourg already has an official preparatory route plus multiple live private alternatives, so the market is not empty even if A9's exact bundle shape may still be different.

1. Jurisdiction scope: Luxembourg
2. Official nationality route: language certificate plus Vivre ensemble certificate
3. Official exam and credential: INLL Sproochentest, A2 speaking, B1 listening, only recognised certification
4. Official integration route: 24-hour Vivre ensemble course or exam with English online delivery
5. Conditional reimbursement route: EUR 75 test fee plus post-application reimbursement path for test and approved-course fees
