## Immediate risk picture

1. [high] No one else can create Luxembourg language curricula at this quality.
   Reason: INLL already offers Luxembourgish courses and the official LAF certification route, and the LLLC also offers broad continuing education. The market is not empty.
   Sources: inll_laf, inll_courses, lllc
2. [medium] Curriculum packages are ready for third-party licensing as-is.
   Reason: Luxembourg guidance says training to third parties outside the company can require a business permit, so the route is not frictionless.
   Sources: guichet_training_establishment
