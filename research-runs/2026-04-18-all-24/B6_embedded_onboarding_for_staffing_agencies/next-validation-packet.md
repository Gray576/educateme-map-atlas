# B6 Validation Packet

Product: `B6 Embedded Onboarding for Staffing Agencies`

## Objective

Decide whether B6 is a real staffing-agency product line with measurable uplift, or only a services hypothesis.

## Current blocked items

- commercial model
- pricing uplift
- market size
- product delivery model
- partner route requirement

## Workstreams

### 1. Buyer-model validation

Questions:
- Do agencies want this as an add-on, a bundled placement component, or a separate service line?
- Would they pay directly, or only tolerate it if bundled inside placement economics?
- Is the Netherlands in first-wave scope, or should it move to expansion because its route differs?

Acceptable evidence:
- 5 buyer calls with staffing or relocation agencies
- one written summary per call capturing buying model, scope, and objections

### 2. Partner / delivery-route validation

Questions:
- Does the offer need an external partner, accreditation owner, or route owner to be credible?
- Can it be delivered as a standalone onboarding product without certification claims?

Acceptable evidence:
- written answer from any required partner or route owner
- documented product architecture decision: standalone, bundled, or partner-led

### 3. Commercial proof

Questions:
- Is the `€1,500` uplift real in observed deals?
- What attach-rate is realistic?
- Is there any primary-source market-size number worth retaining?

Acceptable evidence:
- pilot economics from a real deal or credible buyer-provided estimate
- invoice, proposal, pilot scope, or written buyer confirmation
- primary-source market-size data if numeric sizing stays in the card

## Pass criteria

Pass to `review`:
- product model chosen and buyer-validated at least directionally
- partner/accreditation requirement clarified
- unsupported market-size and uplift numbers removed unless evidenced

Pass to `launchable`:
- one pilot or paid engagement
- real attach-rate or bundled-fee evidence
- confirmed route and delivery model

Kill trigger:
- buyers do not want this as a distinct product and no partner route makes it materially more credible
