## Immediate risk picture

1. [high] Only provider combining EU AI Act compliance + hands-on practice + audit documentation.
   Reason: House of Training already offers similar Luxembourg AI courses with AI Act framing and hands-on usage content.
   Sources: houseoftraining_ai_fundamentals, houseoftraining_ai_act_course, houseoftraining_ai_risk
2. [medium] EU AI Act requires AI literacy from employees.
   Reason: The legal text is narrower: providers and deployers must take measures to ensure sufficient AI literacy of relevant staff and people acting on their behalf, rather than every employee in every company.
   Sources: eu_ai_literacy_qa
3. [medium] Need to quickly train the team to be compliant by Aug 2026.
   Reason: The obligation already applies from 2 February 2025, while August 2026 is the start of supervision and enforcement.
   Sources: eu_ai_literacy_qa, eu_ai_act_start
