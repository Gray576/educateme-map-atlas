# Route-Owner Outreach Template

Use for `A5`, `B2`, `B5`, `B6` when the blocker is route validity, certificate acceptance, accreditation, or funding fit.

## Email template

Subject: Clarification request on route / certificate applicability for `[PRODUCT]`

Hello,

I am validating a training or onboarding offer for `[TARGET AUDIENCE]` and need to confirm the exact route language we are allowed to use.

Could you please clarify the following:

1. Does this route require an authority-recognised exam, certificate, accreditation, or partner status?
2. Would a provider-issued course certificate alone be sufficient for the relevant route or recognition step?
3. If not, what exact outcome wording is safe and accurate?
4. Are there any conditions, exclusions, or limitations that should appear in customer-facing copy?

If possible, a written reply or the exact public document/page to rely on would be sufficient.

Thank you.

## Evidence acceptance

Accept as valid:
- written reply from regulator, route owner, accredited provider, or official administrator
- public page or PDF that answers the route question directly
- sample certificate or programme description tied to the accepted route

Reject as insufficient:
- generic sales copy without route specificity
- third-party blog summaries
- verbal answers with no written follow-up
