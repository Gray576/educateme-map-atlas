# Buyer Interview Template

Use for `A5`, `B2`, `B5`, `B6`, and later `C1` launch validation.

## Interview goal

Determine whether the buyer has a real pain, would buy the proposed packaging, and what evidence would count as a pass or kill signal.

## Core questions

1. What is the current workflow you use for this problem today?
2. Where does the current process fail: compliance, language proof, route navigation, onboarding, or conversion?
3. Would you pay for this as:
   - a standalone service
   - an add-on
   - a bundled component
4. What exact outcome would you need to see before paying?
5. What would make this unsafe or unusable for you?
6. If this were available now, what budget owner and buying process would apply?
7. Would you consider a pilot? If yes, under what scope and success metric?

## Minimum capture format

- buyer role
- company type
- country / route context
- current workaround
- explicit willingness-to-pay signal
- objections
- pilot / LOI potential

## Evidence acceptance

Accept as valid:
- written follow-up email
- pilot scope
- pricing discussion with budget owner
- LOI or trial commitment

Reject as insufficient:
- positive but vague verbal interest
- compliments with no buying path
