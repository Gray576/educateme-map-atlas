# Facebook Operator Pass Review

Scope:

- source: logged-in Facebook session in Chrome
- method: direct Facebook group-search URLs + screen OCR
- purpose: strengthen or weaken `social_funnel_signal` for products with uncertain operator evidence

## Useful positive signals

### Luxembourgish / integration lane

Queries:

- `sproochentest luxembourg`
- `luxembourgish learning`
- `expats in luxembourg`

Observed:

- `Luxembourgish learning`
- `Luxembourg Training, Education, Language Courses Luxembourg`
- `Tandem Luxembourg`
- `Luxembourgish coach/language exchange partner`
- `Expats World in Luxembourg`
- `Luxembourg Expats (Official)`

Takeaway:

- there is a real community layer around Luxembourgish learning and Luxembourg newcomer problems
- this supports top-of-funnel demand for `A9`, `L2`, and `A6`
- it does not, by itself, prove a paid bundle-led funnel

### Nurse relocation / recruitment lane

Query:

- `international nurse recruitment germany`

Observed:

- `Nursing in Germany`
- `Registered Nurses for Germany`
- `Ausbildung and Nursing Job in Germany (Direct Placement in Germany)`

Takeaway:

- there is a clear social-acquisition surface around nurse relocation and Germany placement
- this is useful for `B5` and `B6`
- OCR did not verify which groups are operator-owned or tied to paid offers

## Mostly negative or weak signals

### French-at-work / sector-specific French

Queries:

- `french in luxembourg`
- `french for nurses`
- `medical french`

Observed:

- general Luxembourg/expat groups
- broad nursing groups
- broad medical/IMG groups

Takeaway:

- no clean Facebook proof of a niche operator-led French-for-work or medical-French community
- this supports keeping `L3`, `A7`, and `B2` conservative

### AI compliance / KI training

Queries:

- `ai act compliance`
- `ki schulung`

Observed:

- generic AI groups
- irrelevant `Einschulung` noise on the German query

Takeaway:

- no useful Facebook funnel signal for `B3`
- the operator summary should remain academy-led rather than community-led

## Operational note

The Facebook pass improved calibration, not raw market size estimation.

Useful effect:

- confirms that some lanes have real social-acquisition surfaces
- shows where the market is only broad community noise
- prevents overstating fragmented operator proof where none is visible
