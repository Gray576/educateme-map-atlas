# B5 Validation Packet

Product: `B5 Healthcare Recruitment Pipeline`

## Objective

Prove that B5 is a real buyable service with a valid route and not just a plausible market narrative.

## Current blocked items

- product certificate acceptance
- commercial model
- pricing benchmark
- Erasmus route
- unified Benelux route

## Workstreams

### 1. Route design validation

Questions:
- Is the offer tied to an accepted exam or recognised proof path, or only to internal training completion?
- Should Belgium and Luxembourg be handled as separate workflows?
- What exact route language is safe for each country?

Acceptable evidence:
- written route-owner confirmation
- country-specific compliance notes tied to official pages
- final route map that does not collapse Luxembourg and Belgium into one process

### 2. Buyer validation

Questions:
- Would agencies or hospital employers pay for this exact bundle?
- Which part of the bundle is the real buyer pain: language proof, route navigation, relocation support, or all of them?
- Is this a core paid service or only an optional add-on?

Acceptable evidence:
- 5 buyer interviews minimum
- at least 1 pilot scope, LOI, or written willingness to trial

### 3. Pricing and funding validation

Questions:
- Is there any defendable Benelux price anchor for this type of service?
- Is any funding route actually usable for this commercial structure?

Acceptable evidence:
- public Benelux price anchors or direct buyer quotes
- written funding-route confirmation, or explicit decision to remove funding language

## Pass criteria

Pass to `review`:
- route split resolved
- certificate/exam claim made precise or removed
- pricing and Erasmus wording either evidenced or deleted
- commercial model narrowed to what buyers say they would actually buy

Pass to `launchable`:
- at least 1 buyer pilot or LOI
- route-owner confirmation that the promised outcome path is operationally valid
- unit economics that work at the tested packaging level

Kill trigger:
- no buyer demand for the bundle and no route-recognised outcome path
