## What survived

1. The core Article 4 framing survived in a narrower official form: providers and deployers of AI systems must ensure sufficient AI literacy for relevant staff and other persons acting on their behalf.
2. The compliance story survived only with stronger caveats: the Commission says measures are context-sensitive and does not impose one mandatory training format, sector template, certificate, or governance structure.
3. The timing survived after fail-closed rewrite: the obligation already applies from 2 February 2025, while enforcement begins in August 2026.
4. A real paid French substitute exists through House of Training.
5. A real free multilingual EU-wide substitute exists through Elements of AI, and its course description confirms online completion with no attendance requirements or exams.
